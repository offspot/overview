# Preppers

## **Purpose & Audience**
The pack provides **offline resources for emergency preparedness, survival skills and self-sufficiency**. It is designed for preppers, homesteaders, educators and anyone interested in resilience, DIY solutions and off-grid living. The content covers topics such as food storage, medical care, gardening, technical repairs and privacy tools.
